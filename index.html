Game Target

You Control Pepe, he does not like Chicken very much. But when Chickens are Flat, rubbed with some Salsa and freshly Grilled He loves them! But when Pepe does that, the Chicken Mama gets Really Angry!

So collect some Bottles and Coins to prevent Chicken Mama from hurting Pepe as revenge!

Jump on Small Chickens to collect extra Coins - Kill The Boss Chicken with Bottles
