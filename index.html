El Pollo Loco

About - El Pollo Loco

Game Target

You Control Pepe, he does not like Chicken very much. But when Chickens are Flat, rubbed with some Salsa and freshly Grilled He loves to eat them! But when Pepe does that, the Chicken Mama gets Really Angry!

So collect some Bottles and Coins to prevent Chicken Mama from eating Pepe as revenge!

Controls

To Move Left or Right use A or D, < or > Keys
To Jump use Space Bar
To Sprint use Shift Keys
To Throw Bottles use G or 0 Keys
